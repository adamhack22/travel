  • Pedestal Access

    Explore the pedestal with panoramic
    views of New York Harbor.

    Price: $18.50

  • Crown Access

    Climb to the crown for a
    unique and spectacular view.

    Price: $21.50

  • Ellis Island Immigration Museum

    Visit the museum to learn
    about the history of American immigration.

    Price: Included with Liberty Island ticket

  • Guided Tours

    Take a guided tour for detailed
    information about the statue and its history.

    Price: $35.00
